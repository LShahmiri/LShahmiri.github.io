##  Project Overview
This project presents an **HR Analytics Dashboard** designed to analyse employee data and workforce trends within an organisation.  
The dashboard provides insights into employee attrition, job satisfaction, department distribution, and demographic patterns.

The objective is to help HR teams understand workforce behaviour and support data-driven decision making.

---

##  Key Insights
- Total employees and active workforce overview
- Employee attrition count and attrition rate
- Department-wise attrition analysis
- Job satisfaction distribution by job role
- Employee age group distribution
- Attrition patterns by gender and age group
- Education field analysis related to attrition

---

##  Dashboard Features
- Interactive filters for education level
- Visual analysis of attrition by department
- Job satisfaction heatmap by job role
- Employee demographic breakdown
- Gender-based attrition comparison
